What BaggedUp Does

Every tool a disc
golfer needs. Free.

From your first putter to a fully optimised tournament bag — BaggedUp grows with your game.

📊
Flightpath Simulator

See exactly how every disc in your bag flies — adjusted for your arm speed, throwing hand, and wind conditions. Path mode and stability matrix included.

🤖
AI Bag Coach

Describe your game in plain English. The AI analyses your skill level, arm speed, and bag gaps then recommends exactly the right discs to add or swap.

🧠
My Coach — AI Trainer

Ask anything about technique, shot shaping, course strategy, or grip. A personal AI disc golf coach that understands your game and answers instantly.

🔍
Bag Gap Analysis

Automatically detects missing shot shapes — understable fairways, overstable approach, dedicated putter — and suggests discs from the full database to fill them.

🌬
Wind Simulation

Set headwind, tailwind, or crosswind speed and watch every flightpath in your bag update instantly. Know which disc to throw before you step up to the tee.

📍
Lost Disc GPS

Pin the exact location where you last had a disc. The coordinates are saved to your account and open in Google Maps with one tap. Never lose a disc for good again.

🤝
Card Mates

Search for other BaggedUp users, add them as Card Mates, and view their full bag and disc collection. Compare setups and bag scores on the leaderboard.

📤
PDF & Image Export

Export your full bag to a branded PDF or PNG — complete with disc list, flight numbers, and your flightpath chart. Share it anywhere.

🔥
Mold Slot Heatmap

A visual 14×3 grid shows which speed and stability combinations you're covering — and where you have dangerous gaps or wasteful overlaps.

How It Works

Set up in minutes.

No app store. No credit card. Works on any phone or desktop. Install it like an app or use it in your browser.

01
Create Your Account

Sign up free with your email. Set your skill level, throwing hand, and arm speed in settings. Takes under 2 minutes.

02
Add Your Discs

Search the factory disc database or add community discs. Every disc you own goes into your collection — then assign them to bags.

03
Simulate Your Flights

Watch your flightpath chart populate instantly. Toggle wind, BH/FH view, and stability matrix to understand every disc in your bag.

04
Ask the AI Coach

Get personalised disc recommendations, technique tips, and bag optimisation from an AI that knows your exact setup and skill level.
