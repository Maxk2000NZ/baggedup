BaggedUp started as a personal frustration — and turned into the most complete free disc golf bag management tool available anywhere.

The Problem We Solved

Every disc golfer hits the same wall. You're buying discs, building a bag, and you genuinely don't know if your bag makes sense. You don't know if your discs are too fast for your arm. You don't know what shot shapes you're missing. You don't know how your discs actually fly at your skill level — not how they fly on YouTube for a 450ft pro.

The tools that existed were either too complicated, cost money, or only covered one part of the problem. Flight charts, bag builders, and coaching advice were scattered across different apps and websites, none of them connected.

BaggedUp brings it all together. Flightpath simulation, AI bag coaching, gap analysis, arm speed advisory, lost disc tracking, card mate comparisons, and export tools — all in one free app that runs on any phone or browser.
