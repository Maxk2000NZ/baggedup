🥏 About BaggedUp

BaggedUp started with a simple frustration — there was no good tool to actually understand your bag, simulate how your discs fly, and get honest feedback on what you're missing. So we built one.

Our Mission

Make every disc golfer throw smarter.

Disc golf has a knowledge gap problem. Players buy discs they can't throw, build bags with overlapping coverage, and struggle to understand why their disc went left. BaggedUp closes that gap.

Every tool in BaggedUp — the flightpath simulator, the AI coach, the bag gap analyser, the wind simulator — was designed to give any player the insight that used to require years of experience or a personal coach.

And it's completely free. Always.

The BaggedUp Principles

🆓
Free Forever

No paywalls. No premium tiers. No ads. Every feature in BaggedUp is available to every player from day one — because disc golf shouldn't cost more than it already does.

🎯
Honest Advice

The AI coach tells you the truth. If your arm speed can't handle that driver, it says so. If your bag has a gap you keep ignoring, it won't let you forget. No flattery — just accurate feedback.

📱
Works Everywhere

On the course, at home, on a desktop or a 5-year-old phone. BaggedUp is a Progressive Web App — install it like a native app or use it in your browser. No app store required.

The Story

Why We Built This

Disc golf is one of the fastest-growing sports in the world. But the tools available to players — especially beginners — haven't kept up. Most disc golf apps are basic bag lists. None of them tell you why a disc flew the way it did, or what you should throw next.

BaggedUp was built to change that. We wanted an app that felt like having a knowledgeable friend on the course with you — one who knew your exact bag, understood physics, and could give you honest, specific advice without charging you for it.

The result is an AI-powered platform that combines flightpath simulation, bag analysis, coaching, and disc discovery in one place. It's built for the player standing on the tee wondering which disc to throw — and for the player at home wondering why they keep missing left.

Every feature exists to answer a real question disc golfers actually have. That's the only filter we use.
